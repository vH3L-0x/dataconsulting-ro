Audit GDPR și Analiza Decalajelor

Service Overview

Our GDPR Audit & Gap Analysis service provides a thorough assessment of your organization's data protection practices against GDPR requirements. We identify compliance gaps and provide actionable recommendations to strengthen your privacy framework.

Compliance Assessment

Comprehensive evaluation of current GDPR compliance status.

  • Policy and procedure review
  • Documentation assessment
  • Process evaluation
  • Control effectiveness

Gap Analysis

Identification of areas requiring improvement for full compliance.

  • Compliance gaps identification
  • Risk level assessment
  • Priority areas mapping
  • Improvement opportunities

Action Planning

Development of strategic remediation plans.

  • Remediation roadmap
  • Resource planning
  • Timeline development
  • Implementation guidance

Reporting & Documentation

Detailed documentation of findings and recommendations.

  • Comprehensive audit report
  • Executive summary
  • Detailed recommendations
  • Progress tracking tools
